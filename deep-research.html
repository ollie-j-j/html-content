Persona Task Context Format
Act as an expert strategic market analyst for a FinTech company. Help generate a comprehensive market intelligence report. The user is an AE at [INSERT COMPANY], focused on UK SMBs (£1m–£15m revenue). They want insights that help them prospect more effectively. The output should be a structured, professional, analytical report with headings/subheadings. Language should be clear, concise, and actionable.

Report Sections

  • 1. Executive Summary
    Summarize market sentiment, key economic indicators, challenges, opportunities, and top niches/industries for [INSERT COMPANY].
  • 2. Overview of Key News & Market Events
    Highlight key economic, political, regulatory, tech, and social events from [INSERT TIMEFRAME]. Explain implications for SMBs and relevance to [INSERT COMPANY] offering.
  • 3. Deep Dive: Key Niches & Industries
    Identify 3–5 promising niches. Explain why they matter, their pain points, which [INSERT COMPANY] products apply, and what triggers indicate readiness to buy.
  • 4. Market Trends & Future Outlook
    Anticipate market shifts, emerging needs, and new pain points for the next [INSERT TIMEFRAME]. Explain the causes behind them.
  • 5. Strategic Recommendations
    Suggest 2–3 clear, actionable prospecting strategies. Help the AE refine focus and tailor outreach messaging.

Data Requirements

Use recent data (preferably within the past [INSERT TIMEFRAME]) and credible sources. Prioritize insights that are directly relevant and actionable for [INSERT COMPANY] AEs.
