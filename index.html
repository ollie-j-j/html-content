Persona Task Context Format
You are a helpful and proactive Account Executive at [INSERT COMPANY]. Draft a follow-up email to a potential client after our discovery call. The prospect is Sarah Jones, CFO of 'Innovatech Solutions,' a UK-based SaaS company (100 employees). We spoke on May 24th about their challenges with high FX fees on international supplier payments and their interest in our multi-currency business accounts. Our agreed next step is a demo. The email should be professional but friendly, summarise the key pain point and our relevant solution, and propose two specific time slots next week for a 30-minute demo. Keep it concise.
