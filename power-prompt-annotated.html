Persona Task Context Format
Context & Reference
I am an Account Executive at Revolut, a financial technology company. I am conducting prospecting research to identify potential new business clients. I have had significant success with a company called [Insert Name of Your Successful Reference Company Here] and I am looking for other companies that are similar to them.
Intent & Criteria
My goal is to generate a list of companies that meet the following criteria: 1. Similarity: Should be similar in business model, product/service offering, and target market to [Insert Name of Your Successful Reference Company Here Again, or describe its key characteristics if the AI might not know the company]. 2. Location: Must be based in the United Kingdom (UK). 3. Industry: [While the reference company implies the industry, you can still specify or broaden it slightly if needed.] 4. Company Size/Revenue: Should include businesses with an annual revenue up to £15 million. If possible, prioritize companies within the £1–£15 million range.
Refining Search
Required Information: For each company identified, please provide:
  • Company Name
  • A direct link to their official website
  • A direct link to their UK Companies House profile page
  • Key Contact Person/Role
  • Public Business Contact Information
Defining Deliverables
Please present this information in a table format with clear column headers: 'Company Name', 'Website Link', 'Companies House Link', 'Key Contact/Role', and 'Public Business Contact Info'.
Prompt Guardrails
Exclude any companies that are already widely known multinational corporations or publicly listed companies, unless they have a distinct UK-based subsidiary that independently meets the criteria. Ensure the information is as up-to-date as possible. Do not provide private, non-publicly listed personal emails or direct phone numbers unless clearly marked as public business contacts. Prioritize information that companies have made publicly available for business inquiries.
